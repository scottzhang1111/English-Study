　　　　　　　　Emma’s Old Toy

　Emma is a high school student.
One day, she went to a flea market with her friends.
The market was very big, and so many items were being sold.
While they were walking around, Emma found ( 21(something familiar) ) at one of the shops.
It looked very similar to the old toy she used to play with when she was little.
So, she picked it up, and soon, she was very surprised because she found her name on it.

　Then, a little boy came up to Emma.
She immediately knew from his face that he ( 22(wanted her old toy) ).
So, she passed it to him and told him to take good care of it.
She could not believe she had found her old toy by chance after so many years.
She could not stop smiling for a while because it reminded her of so many old memories that she had.

　エマは高校生です。

すぐに、そのおもちゃに自分の名前が書いてあるのを見つけてとても驚きました。

　すると、男の子がエマに近づいてきました。

その子の顔を見てわかりました。

長い年月を経て、偶然にも自分の古いおもちゃを見つけたことが信じられませんでした。
たくさんの昔の思い出がよみがえってきて、しばらくの間、彼女は微笑みを浮かべ続けていました。
