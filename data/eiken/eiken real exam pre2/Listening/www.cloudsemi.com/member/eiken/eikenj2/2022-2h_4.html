What did Alan do last Saturday?

問34 Question:

答え： ( 35 )

答え： ( 36 )
(1) He quit his job as manager.
(2) He went on a trip to Ireland.
(3) He started eating more ginger.
(4) He stopped drinking champagne.

答え： ( 37 )
(1) because other drinks give them stomachaches.
