What is one thing that Grandpa says to Peter?

(1) It is not possible for him to stay for longer than a week.

問32 Question:

(1) whether he can run quickly.
(2) whether he has ever gone fishing.
(3) if he knows how to do any magic tricks.
(4) if he has gone camping before.

問34 Question:
What is one thing that we learn about Richard Hollingshead's mother?

答え： ( 34 )
(1) She made a drive-in movie theater in her yard.
(2) She learned how to drive a car by watching movies.
(3) She often held parties for her family and neighbors.
(4) She thought movie theater seats were not comfortable.
問35 Question:
One reason that drive-in movie theaters
became popular was (1) or (2) or (3) or (4)

答え： ( 35 )
(1) they offered special discounts to families with children.
(2) parents did not have to worry if their children were noisy.
(3) most indoor movie theaters did not show movies for children.
(4) many of them were built near parks with children's playgrounds.
問36 Question:
Some movies were not shown in drive-in movie theaters
because (1) or (2) or (3) or (4)

答え： ( 36 )
(1) it was too dark in the evening to see the movies easily.
(2) the sound in the movies was not good enough.
(3) movie companies made more money from indoor theaters.
(4) they had not been popular in indoor theaters.
問37 Question:
Why did many drive-in movie theater owners sell their theaters?

答え： ( 37 )
(1) Companies offered to pay them a lot of money for their land.
(2) The theaters were too far away from large towns and cities.
(3) They wanted to open stores so that people could rent videos.
(4) People started making drive-in theaters in their own yards.
