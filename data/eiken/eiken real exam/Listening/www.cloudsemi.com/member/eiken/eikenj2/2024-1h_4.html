1-4 英語検定(準２級)　筆記 2024年第1回(No.26～No.29) 各１０点 × ４問 ＝ ４０点満点
次の英文を読み、(26)から(29)の各質問に最も適切なものを、1～4 の中から一つ選びなさい。

問26 Question:
What is one thing that local newspapers do for their communities?

答え： ( 26 )
(1) They teach local people how to write articles and take photographs.
(2) They regularly organize events for people who live in the area.
(3) They give information about what is happening around the world.
(4) They offer chances to work for people who live in the area.
問27 Question:
Because more people read the news online these days, (1) or (2) or (3) or (4)

答え： ( 27 )
(1) the number of news stories that they read has decreased.
(2) people have less time to do other important activities.
(3) fewer people know about things that are happening nearby.
(4) the number of local newspapers has gone up since the 1990s.
問28 Question:
Local newspaper companies have found it hard to create news websites
because (1) or (2) or (3) or (4)

答え： ( 28 )
(1) they have to stay open twentyfour hours every day.
(2) they often cannot do it without the help of experts.
(3) many of their workers have left their jobs.
(4) many of their readers are not able to use the Internet.
問29 Question:
What is one thing that has changed for small, local businesses?

答え： ( 29 )
(1) They are not able to advertise as easily as they did in the past.
(2) They cannot sell the same kinds of products as they used to.
(3) They have found many more customers by setting up websites.
(4) They have become very important sources of local news.
