問31 Question:
What is one thing that Ralph asks Gary?

答え： ( 31 )
(1) If Gary has tried the basketball court in his local park.
(2) If Gary bought a new basketball when he went to the mall.
(3) Whether Gary's new school is near his new house.
(4) Whether Gary's parents are planning to move to a new house.
問32 Question:

答え： ( 32 )
(1) will play in a basketball tournament in June.
(2) have told him about a great new board game.
(3) want to know if Gary can remember them.
(4) came to stay with his family last year.

(2) Tell Ralph the best dates to come.

(4) Visit Ralph's relatives in the city.

Why did some students put computers in places like bars and cafés?

答え： ( 35 )
(1) To discover how much money people would pay for a computer.
(2) To do research on why computer games had become so popular.
(3) So that they could ﬁnd out what food and drinks customers bought.
(4) So that they could get some money from the games they had made.
問36 Question:

答え： ( 36 )
(1) that they could get to know new people.
(2) that they thought space monsters might attack.
(3) to show people the games they had created.
(4) to get jobs making computer game machines.
問37 Question:
