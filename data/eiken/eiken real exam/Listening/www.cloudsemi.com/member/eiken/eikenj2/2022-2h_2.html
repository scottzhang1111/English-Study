問11 （　　）に入れるのに最も適切なものを選びなさい。

A : Brian, I think the new boy at school is really cute,
but I don't know his name.

B : He's in my gym class.
I'll find ( 　　) his name for you.

答え：
(1) out
(2) up
(3) above
(4) away

(3) bring up
