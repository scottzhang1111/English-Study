問16 （　　）に入れるのに最も適切なものを選びなさい。

Son : Yes, please!

(2) How many did you have?
(3) Do you have enough time?
(4) Would you like to try one?
問17 （　　）に入れるのに最も適切なものを選びなさい。

Girl 1 : Good idea.

(2) I didn't bring my watch.
(3) Let's walk for a while.
(4) I started four years ago.
問18 （　　）に入れるのに最も適切なものを選びなさい。

(2) Don't go there.
(3) Stop practicing the piano.
(4) Say hello to the teacher for me.
