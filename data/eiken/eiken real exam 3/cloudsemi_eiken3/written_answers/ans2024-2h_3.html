【3C】
　　　　　　　　The SS Savannah

　The SS Savannah was an American ship.
It was built in the 1810s.
(30-1)It was planned as a sailing ship for people and cargo.
The design had large rooms with carpets and curtains.
However, a change was made to the ship’s design.
At that time, people were developing steam engines.
(26)So, the SS Savannah became a sailing ship that had a steam engine, too.

　The ship’s captain also had an idea for the SS Savannah.
He wanted to take it across the Atlantic Ocean to England.
This plan worried some people because England was far away.
(27)They thought such a ship could not travel so far.
However, the captain found some people who wanted to join him,
and he began the dangerous trip.

　The SS Savannah arrived in England after about one month.
For most of the trip, the SS Savannah used sail power,
but it also sometimes used steam power.
(30-2)It was the first ship to use steam power to help it to cross the Atlantic Ocean.
(28)Many people in England went to see this ship.
Before the SS Savannah went home, it went to other countries such as Sweden.

　The trip was a success.
However, some people still thought steam technology was dangerous.
The ship’s steam engine was taken out.
The SS Savannah was then used as a sailing ship to take people
and cargo between Georgia and New York.
(29)(30-3)Sadly, in the early 1820s, the SS Savannah had an accident near New York City
and broke into many pieces.
　　　　　　SS Savannah 号

　SS サバンナ号はアメリカの船である。
1810年代に建造された。
(30-1)人と貨物のための帆船として計画された。
絨毯とカーテンが敷かれた広い部屋が設計されていた。
しかし、船の設計に変更が加えられた。
当時、人々は蒸気機関を開発していた。
(26)そこでSSサバンナ号は、蒸気機関も備えた帆船となった。

　船長はまた、SSサバンナ号にあるアイデアを持っていた。
サバンナ号で大西洋を横断し、イギリスまで行こうというのだ。
この計画は、イギリスが遠かったため、一部の人々を心配させた。
(27)彼らは、そんな船がそんなに遠くまで行けるはずがないと思ったのだ。
しかし、船長は何人かの仲間を見つけ、危険な旅を始めた。

　SSサバンナ号は約1ヵ月後にイギリスに到着した。

(30-2)大西洋を横断するために蒸気動力を使用した最初の船だった。
